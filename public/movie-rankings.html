The Best Movies According to Robert

My top 3 Movies of All-Time

The Departed

A movie showing the true nature of the Irish Mafia in the southern part of Boston, MA, known as "Southie" and how an undercover cop risked his life to take them down.

Star Wars

A true classic and inovation of creativitey displaying an intergalactic war in space between many planets between the evil Empire and the loyal Republic in and intergalactic scale war.

Saving Private Ryan

A truely horrific, gruesome, but realistic dipiction of what World War 2 veterans had to go through to invade Normandy to stop Hitler's ruthless onslaught of attempting world domination.
